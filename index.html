ABOUT ME

Web developer, and former systems engineer who loves music and dancing to keep fit.

Whether while voluntaring or at work, I am always drawn to helping others, through encouragement, support or mentorship.

This desire to find ways to help individuals and communities is refelected in many of my projects, which seek to uncover services which people will find of genuine value.

I have always had a love of technology and a desire and passion to create; hence my decision to pursue a career in engineering. This has allowed me to contribute to solutions that have had a tangible impact on peoples lives. Over the years I have learnt to effectively deliver solutions enabled by new and legacy technologies through the integration of resources; applying skills in project and programme management. However my passion to create remains, and now I am looking for opportunities to help people and support the growth of business through the development of services enabled by the web. Hence my decision to complete an immersive programme in full stack web development.
